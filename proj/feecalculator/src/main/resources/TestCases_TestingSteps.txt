Test Cases:
----------
1) Check The number of records processed in 20 and stored in list is 20 or not as source file has 20 valid records( should be 20)
2) Check InraDay record fee in report is 10 or not
3) if Not TraDay record and pripority 'Y' or 'y' then fee 500 or not
4) if Not TraDay record and pripority 'n' or 'N' and Transaction type (sell or withdraw) then fee 100 or not
5) if Not TraDay record and pripority 'n' or 'N' and Transaction type (buy or deposit) then fee 50 or not

6) Currupt the record in sourcefile change '|' or ',' for record to = then processed records shoud be 19 in list.
7) Currupt the record in sourcefile change '|' or ',' for record to = then it shas to throw InvalidTransactionException exception
8) Check the report is showing in sorted order
